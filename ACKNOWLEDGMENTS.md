### Inspiration
This project was inspired by the challenges faced by Kenyan farmers in:
- Accessing real-time weather information
- Making informed planting decisions
- Managing crop health and pest control
- Understanding market prices and profitability
- Optimizing irrigation and resource usage

## Impact Goals

The Climate Crop Monitor aims to:
1. Improve agricultural productivity through data-driven decisions
2. Reduce crop losses from pests, diseases, and adverse weather
3. Enhance farmer income through better market information
4. Promote sustainable farming practices
5. Bridge the digital divide in rural agricultural communities
